SASHA SLOAN

ABOUT

Sasha Sloan is a singer and songwriter based in Los Angeles.

The artist wrote songs for Camila Cabello, Katy Perry, and John Legend.

She uses music as an outlet to be open and vulnerable.

In October 16, 2020, Sasha Sloan released her debut album called "Only Child."

She wrote about 30 to 50 songs and narrowed it down to 10.

One of her songs, "Is it Just Me?" was written over Zoom.
